our story

Step into the world of ADXC, where the planet’s most formidable fighters unite in the vibrant core of Abu Dhabi.

Imagine a single, electrifying cage where the grace of Judo, the force of Sumo, the technique of Sambo, the elegance of Jiu-Jitsu, the power of Wrestling (both Freestyle and Greco-Roman), and the cunning of Submission Grappling all collide in a dance of strength and skill.

ADXC isn’t just about competition; it’s a celebration of Abu Dhabi’s steadfast support for athletes. We lead the charge with revolutionary martial arts concepts, spotlighting the city as a global sports titan and an engine for international economic growth. .

This isn’t merely a platform; it’s a launchpad for talent. Here, athletes and businesses come together, exchanging wisdom and fueling the future of martial arts.

ADXC revolutionizes the sport with inventive rules and unique concepts, delivering an unparalleled spectacle.

Our captivating fight card format features exclusively invited athletes vying for prestigious titles and glory. So join us in this unique story where legends are born and legacies are engraved in history.

Judo

Sumo

Sambo

Ju-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)

Wrestling Freestyle and Greco-Roman

Submission Grappling

These diverse influences come together to create a rich and dynamic gaming experience.

ruleset

Bouts consist of: 3X3 minutes rounds for Prelims and Main Cards 5×3 minutes round for Main Event and Co Main Event with 1 minute break in between.

The matches feature a dynamic format, with athletes winning via tap-out, verbal tap-out, referee stoppage due to imminent danger or referee decision..

Athletes can attempt throws, takedowns, and both standing and ground submissions until one gives up or the time limit is reached.

If no submission is achieved, the match is then judged using a 10-point must system similar to MMA.
